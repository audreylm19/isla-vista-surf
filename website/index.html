Isla Vista Wave Quality

Analyzing swell, tide, and wind data in Isla Vista from 2017 to 2021 to see if there is a noticeable trend in wave quality.

All of the plotting is done in Bokeh. I'm still working on the final visualization—I need to learn how to use CustomJS callbacks—but the idea is to click on a bar in the simple stacked bar chart to see the corresponding year's hover graph.

In the hover graph, each point represents a daylight hour. You can roll over the graph to see an hour's conditions and surf quality score.
